Guest Information

Everything you need to know before joining us.

We request smart-casual attire. While we celebrate traditional clothing from all cultures, we kindly ask guests to refrain from wearing beachwear, sleeveless shirts (for men), or athletic wear.

While walk-ins are always welcome at our Calicut and Kochi locations, reservations are highly recommended for our UAE and Oman branches, especially during weekends and holidays.

Complimentary valet parking is available at our Downtown Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Muscat locations. For our India locations, dedicated secure parking is provided on-site.
