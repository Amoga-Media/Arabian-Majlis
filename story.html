Beyond the Plate

Our Roots.

It began with a single flame in Calicut.

Before the international expansion, before the accolades, there was just a profound love for the communal dining culture of the Arab world. We wanted to create a 'Majlis'—a place of gathering—right in the heart of Kerala.

We spent years traveling through the Middle East, studying under local masters to bring the exact, uncompromised flavors of authentic Mandi back home.

The Visionary

Shahed Pathan

Led by culinary visionary Shahed Pathan, the journey of Arabian Majlis has always been guided by an uncompromising dedication to heritage. With a background deeply rooted in traditional spice blending, Shahed ensured that scaling to three countries never meant diluting the soul of the food.

"Food is not just sustenance; it is the ultimate storyteller of a culture."

The Expansion

A timeline of our growing family.

2018

The first Arabian Majlis opens its doors in Calicut, introducing the signature Chicken Mandi.

2021

Crossing borders. The first international branch opens in Dubai, testing our roots in the Middle East itself.

2024

Expansion into Oman and scaling to 12 total outlets. The family grows to over 250 staff members.

2025

The introduction of the "Private Majlis" dining experience, bringing ultra-luxury events to our flagship locations.

2026

A new era. Launching our custom digital platforms and expanding the menu to cater to modern, health-conscious diners.
